From your local £1/2 cash game to multi-day tournaments, SharkTrackr captures every detail that matters.

🃏

Cash Game Tracking

Log buy-ins, rebuys, cash-outs, blinds, and stake levels. Track at any venue with full session notes, tags, and a 1–5 star rating system.

🏆

Tournament Tracking

Track entry fees, rake, re-entries, add-ons, bounties, final position, and field size. Full P&L calculated automatically.

🔴

Live Session Mode

Start a live session and update your chip stack in real time. See your running P&L, peak stack, and session duration at a glance.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Visualise your P&L over time with Apple Charts. See your best venues, most profitable game types, and hourly rate trends.

💳

Multi-Currency Bankroll

Separate wallets for any world currency. Record deposits, withdrawals, and cross-currency transfers with exchange rate logging.

📤

CSV Export

Export your full session history to CSV at any time. Your data is always yours — no lock-in, no subscriptions required to access it.

🏠

Venue & Game Management

Build up your own library of venues, game variants, and betting structures. Everything pre-populated with sensible defaults to get you started.

💾

Backup & Restore

Export a full backup of all your data and restore it to any device. Perfect for upgrading your phone without losing your history.

⌛

Apple Watch App

Start and end sessions from your wrist. Log chip stack updates, track live P&L, and record reloads without touching your phone — all synced instantly.
